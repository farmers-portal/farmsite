RABI CROPS

Rabi crops are agricultural crops that are sown in winter and harvested in the spring in India. The crops are grown either with rainwater that has percolated into the ground, or using irrigation.

KHARIF CROPS

In India, the season is popularly considered to start in June and to end in October. Kharif crops are usually sown with the beginning of the first rains during the advent of the south-west monsoon season, and they are harvested at the end of monsoon season.
